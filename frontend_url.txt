Master Remarks

GET {Base URL}dedup/master-remarks/<int:pk>/ — Get details of a specific master remark
patch {Base URL}dedup/master-remarks/<int:pk>/ — Update a master remark
DELETE {Base URL}dedup/master-remarks/<int:pk>/ — Delete a master remark
